Eliott Erwitt

Elliott Erwitt (born Elio Romano Erwitt, July 26, 1928)
is a French-born American advertising and documentary
photographer known for his black and white candid photos
of ironic and absurd situations within everyday settings.

member of Magnum Photos since 1953
